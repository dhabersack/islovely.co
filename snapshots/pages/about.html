About

I have been working on the web since 2005. Since then, I have worked on web-facing projects for a small number of businesses, most of them related to content management systems as well as the migration to new systems.

While I am primarily self-taught, I also have an educational background in the field of Human Computer Interaction.

  • B.Sc. Media Informatics, Human-Computer-Interaction at Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München from October 2011 to July 2014
  • PSD to HTML/CSS
  • extended open source CMS as per client requirements (new functionality such as internationalization for multinational company)
  • platform migrations
  • full-time video editing in tourism industry for eight months
  • editing on multiple machines simultaneously, often under intense pressure
  • direct and personal contact with end-consumers
  • streamlined editing-process, simplified audio- and video-equipment by reorganizing and removing legacy equipment
  • established freelancing business in 2009, have been working with small businesses on improving their online presences
  • phone support at JUMO

Teaching

  • developed a passion for passing my knowledge on to those new to the industry
