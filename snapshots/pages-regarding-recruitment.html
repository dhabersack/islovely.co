Regarding recruitment

I am currently not looking for employment.

My freelancing business allows me to get to know many different industries, and the expertise I gain during these engagements is beneficial to all clients I work with. As such, I believe I can be more valuable as an independent contractor as opposed to a salaried employee.

However, we can certainly talk about a project-based collaboration.

If you would like to hire me for your project, you can request a proposal or contact me directly at dom@islovely.co.

Information for recruitment agencies

I prefer to interact with my clients directly instead of going through intermediaries. Because of this, I do not accept offers presented through recruitment agencies.

Feel free to pass my information on to your client so they can contact me directly.
